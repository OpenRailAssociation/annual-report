## How OpenRail Creates Impact

### Open Source in Production

Since 2023, OSRD has been deployed in production at SNCF Réseau, demonstrating that open source software can power critical railway infrastructure operations. With approximately **100 users** actively accessing the application, OSRD serves multiple use cases including **timetabling, operation studies, infrastructure management, and short-term capacity management**. The platform provides a modern, performant web-based tool for creating timetables with a strong focus on collaboration, replacing traditional approaches with an integrated solution that combines **macroscopic and microscopic railway information**, enabling both high-level planning and detailed operational analysis to work together seamlessly.

A key factor in OSRD's successful production deployment is its **extensibility through custom integrations**. SNCF Réseau has developed private connectors that collect and transform internal data before pushing it to OSRD, while the `railway_manager_interface` allows organization-specific operations such as importing timetables in proprietary formats and exporting train schedules to integrate with their existing IT systems. This architecture demonstrates how open source projects can be smoothly integrated into complex enterprise environments without compromising on specialized requirements.

Beyond immediate operational benefits, OSRD's open source nature supports a strategic vision: establishing a **standard platform** that enables railway companies to collaborate on **European-scale timetable planning**. As most features requested by SNCF Réseau align with needs expressed by other railway operators, the open development model ensures that improvements benefit the entire industry, fostering interoperability and reducing duplication of effort across the European railway sector.
